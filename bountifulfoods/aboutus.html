Welcome to Bountiful Foods. We are a small baking company located in the small town of Carlsbad, California.

Baking has always been close to my heart. I began baking in 2011 with my mother. Our favorite thing to bake together were fresh fruit pies! Baking fresh became something that I loved to do. This business allows me to do what I love and help others discover a love for fresh fruit and baking just as I have.

This rapidly growing business has allowed me to expand to new markets and allowed me to begin a made-to-order buisiness. Try one of our fresh drinks today!
