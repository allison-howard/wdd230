Chamber of Commerce - Happy Valley, OR

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to help further the interests of small businesses in Happy Valley. This can help develop strong local networks and result in business exchanges that develop pro-business initiatives. This site will bring exposure to businesses within the area.

Available Domain:

happyvalleycommerce.com

Logo:

Target Audience:

Who: Any person wanting to get a quick look at what's going on in their hometown and promote or know more about business around them. Ages 10-100, who have access to an Internet connected device.

Personas:

Occupation: Local Resident

Motivation to use the site: Wants to learn about and benefit from local businesses.

Occupation: Local business owner.

Motivation to use the site: Wants to promote and advertise his business and network with other businesses.

Scenarios:

  1. What are some upcoming events in my area?
  2. Being new to the area, I want to learn about the businesses in Happy Valley.
  3. What are some popular businesses in my area?
  4. I want to learn what goes on behind the scenes in my community.
  5. How can I be more involved in the community?
